chatgpt：

A father was about to jump off a building because his son had committed suicide by jumping off the company building twenty days ago. He had been trying to negotiate(谈判) compensation（赔偿） with the company but had not reached an agreement. Today, the father arrived at the third floor of the company’s office building, holding a picture of his child, and sat by the window, preparing to jump. The police arrived at the scene（到达现场） as quickly as possible, and the rescue team and personnel from the family and relevant departments（部门） also arrived in time. The father, in his words, said he didn’t want money, he wanted his child back, and he didn’t let anyone come near. After repeated persuasion（劝说） by the family members, they worked together to pull him away from the window to a safe position. Perhaps all of this was in order to expedite（加快） the negotiation of compensation, but how much is a life worth?

bing AI：

A father wants to jump off a building because his son committed suicide by jumping off the company building twenty days ago. He has been trying to coordinate（协调） compensation with the company but has not reached an agreement. Today, the father came to the third floor of the company office building with his child’s portrait and sat by the window ready to jump off. The police arrived at the scene as quickly as possible, and the rescue team also arrived in time, as well as family members and personnel from relevant departments. The father said that he didn’t want money but wanted people. He didn’t let anyone approach（靠近） him. After repeated persuasion from his family members, he was pulled from the window to a safe position. Perhaps all of this is for the sake of coordinating compensation as soon as possible, but how much is a life worth?
